Chocolate Billionaires

Description

Chocolate billionaires are a delectably rich treat that perfectly blends the creamy texture of chocolate with the crunch of caramel and pecans, creating a luxury in every bite. This indulgent dessert starts with a layer of buttery, crisp toffee, studded with chopped pecans, laying the foundation for the ultimate sweet experience. Over this crunchy base, a smooth and luscious layer of caramel is spread, marrying the textures and flavors in a divine symphony. The masterpiece is then enrobed in a thick coating of silky, melt-in-your-mouth chocolate, often topped with a sprinkle of sea salt to enhance the chocolate's depth and the caramel's sweetness. Chocolate billionaires are not just a treat; they're an experience, embodying opulence and indulgence in every bite, making them a perfect subject for any food blog looking to explore the richer side of sweet treats.

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces caramels
  • 1 cup Rice Krispies
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 3 cups milk chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 cups chooped pecans
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons shortening

Steps

  1. Line 2 baking sheets with waxed paper; grease the paper and set aside.
  2. In a large heavy saucepan, combine the caramels and water; cook and stir over low heat until smooth.
  3. Stir in pecans and cereal until coated. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto prepared pans.
  4. Refrigerate for 10 minutes or until firm.
  5. Microwave, melt chocolate chips and shortening; stir until smooth.
  6. Dip candy into chocolate, coating all sides; allow excess to drip off.
  7. Place on prepared pans. Refrigerate until set.
  8. Store in an airtight container.
