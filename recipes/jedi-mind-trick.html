Jedi Mind Trick

Description

The Jedi Mind Trick cocktail is an enchanting blend that mesmerizes with its complexity and depth, much like the famed ability from the Star Wars saga it's named after. This captivating drink combines the smooth, subtle strength of vodka with the sweet, nuanced flavors of blue curaçao and fresh lemon juice, creating a visually stunning, blue-hued concoction. A splash of grapefruit juice adds a layer of tart sophistication, while a hint of simple syrup balances the force of flavors with just the right amount of sweetness. Served in a highball glass over a bed of ice and garnished with a twist of lemon or a sprig of mint, the Jedi Mind Trick is a refreshing and otherworldly delight that promises to sway the senses and enchant the palate, making it a celestial offering for any food blog exploring the universe of themed cocktails.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 ounces grapefruit and rose vodka
  • 1 ounce white grape juice
  • 1 ounce John D. Taylor’s Velvet Falernum
  • 1 teaspoon Bols Blue Curacao
  • 1 teaspoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Dash of grapefruit bitters

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and shake
  2. Serve on ice and enjoy!
